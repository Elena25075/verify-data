#### Health Score Guide
- **9-10:** Production-ready. No issues or only trivial observations.
- **7-8:** Minor issues. Usable for most purposes, fix warnings for production.
- **5-6:** Significant gaps. Address critical issues before use.
- **3-4:** Major problems. Substantial cleaning/fixing needed.
- **1-2:** Unusable. Fundamental structural or completeness failures.
